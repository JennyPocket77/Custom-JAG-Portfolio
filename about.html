About Jennifer

Hi! I am Jennifer Ann Gonzalez, a New Front-End Developer based in Los Angeles, CA. USA

I have most recently been an Intermediate Commercial & Residential Interior Designer, and Project Manager, with over 9 years professional industry experience. I value continued education, collaboration, & open productive communication. I find inspiration in nature, music, hands-on DIY crafting, gardening, swimming, history, architecture, culture, and unabashed curiosity.

As of 2020 during the Covid-19 Pandemic, I was laid-off from the firm I was working with, yet I allowed my newfound freedom of direction to blosom into an opportunity to completely switch gears, and to learn a new trade! I've been loving immersing myself learning all about coding, web design, graphic design, & small business management!

Working with the SheCodes team within their thorough, fully-loaded suite of hands-on workshops has been invaluable for me! The entire team is patient, thoughtful, knowledgeable, and encouraging. They provided a wonderful environment to kick-off my interest in coding and web design, and I am so grateful for coming across them when I did!

I am currently working on developing my own web design & branding business, Creation Pillar Studio, and working on developing my website at creationpillar.com which has been an exciting leap for me to dive head-first into! Hoping to kick-off my launch after the new year in 2022!
